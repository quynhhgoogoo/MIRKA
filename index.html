about us

Mirka was established in 1943 in Helsinki capital by the engineer Onni Aulo. Nowadays, Mirka is an international organization and a member of the KWH Group.

Pioneering in abrasives industry with the promise of dedicating to the finish, Mirka Ltd also has a big heart in successfully aligning and integrating corporate social responsibility with its business model. Specifically, using Abranet – an advanced patented abrasive tool, customers will feel a sense of dust – free interaction and satisfy with its tool’s efficient and effective performance. Even with smallest, hard - headed particles floating in the air after sanding, Abranet can handle and remove them quickly.

Mirka company protect customer’s welfare, indirectly and directly contribute to improve environment context and continuously educate for the sake of younger generation. The company has proved that future of human life is strongly held in hands by our responsibilities and actions. We must go “dust – free” lifestyle now!

Our vision

“To spread out the dust - free lifestyle with Mirka Oy to protect and educate young generation, who will hold the future”

Do you be aware of the air we all breathe? Well, global warming is on the rise, as same as the air and dust population. Our health could be threatened, our life span could be shortened and be attacked by many diseases. Imagine a day you wake up in a hospital and regret to do nothing to protect the green earth mother. Our young offspring will still be the victims of this tragedy if we do not act now to mitigate and prevent environment. Therefore, instead of being in a human - made pollution, we must act now. Coming from the smallest action, this is using Mirka – dust free products to assure you and your children’s future.
